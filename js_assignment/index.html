How to Play

1. A few tiles will flash briefly

2. Click on the tiles that flashed

3. If you get them all correct, you move on to the next level

4. If you click the wrong tile, you lose a life

5. You have 3 lives, try to reach the highest level possible



Game Difficulty

As you progress, the game will become more challenging

The grid will expand, and the number of flashing tiles will increase

Stay focused, test your memory, and have fun!
